--- title: "Notes on Aphasia" slug: notesonaphasia carousel_image: /assets/images/notesonaphasia/12.jpg main_image: /assets/images/notesonaphasia/IMG_5486.jpg year: 2018 technique: > two looped slide-projections (with 80 slides each),
other materials (boxes, post-its, sheets of paper) brief: > A series of photographs, taken in a textile factory in Italy and in the streets of Munich, constructs the raw material for this visual and narrative flow. Street signs, ads, handwritten instructions, logos, commonly used symbols get deconstructed, fall apart, disintegrate. Sillables and signs repeat and chase one another in this syncronised and rhythmically beaten double projection, where the distinction between form and content, referent and signifier, performative instruction and accidental contingency, imprint and speech simply collapses. Language becomes a failed architecture, an empty shell, a flashing nonsensical hallucination, a tiring and never-fulfilled effort.
(Installation view and details. Photos: Giulia Zabarella, Mathias Zausinger) ---
