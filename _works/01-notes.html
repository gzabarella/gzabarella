--- title: "Notes on aphasia" slug: notes cover_image: /assets/images/notes.png year: 2018 technique: > two looped and sync slide-projections,
plastic boxes, stack of papers, post-its brief: > A series of photographs, taken in a textile factory in Italy and in the streets of Munich, constructs the raw material for this visual and narrative flow. Street signs, ads, handwritten instructions, logos, commonly used symbols get diconstructed, fall apart, disintegrate. Sillables and signs repeat and chase one another in this syncronised and rhythmically beaten double projection, where the distinction between form and content, referent and signifier, performative instruction and accidental contingency, imprint and speech simply collapses. Language becomes a failed architecture, an empty shell, a flashing nonsensical hallucination, a tiring and never-fulfilled effort. ---
